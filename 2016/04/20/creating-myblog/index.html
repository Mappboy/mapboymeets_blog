Like all good programmers in order to create my blog, I borrowed and borrowed hard. But unlike all programmers I must give credit where credit is due. In the source of this page if you look at the footer you will notice the attribution for this blog is commented out. But a large part of the credit for this site belongs to Jonathan Klughertz, whose own site is very useful itself. I have made some slight edits to the clean blog theme to create this site.
Why did I create this site ? Well a full explanation can be found in the FUQ /FUQ, but in short because I wanted to do something with node.js. The secondary reason is because doing something is the best way to learn something, however bad you may be when you start. . I stumbled across hexo.io a lightweight simple blog generator. So I spun up a droplet on Digital Ocean and folllowed their tutorial on setting up the blog.
